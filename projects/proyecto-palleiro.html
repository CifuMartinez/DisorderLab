Palleiro

Born in hospitality. Built to serve it.

This brand doesn’t just supply the hospitality industry — it comes from it. With roots firmly planted in the world of bars, kitchens, and dining rooms, its entire strategy is driven by one truth: they started as hosts, servers, and chefs themselves. That’s why they understand the pulse of the business — what works, what doesn’t, and what’s needed to make it thrive.

Positioning themselves as true allies of the hospitality community, they challenge the cold distance of big market players. Their mission is simple: to stand side by side with the people who make it all happen, offering products, logistics, and support that actually respond to real needs. If their clients win, they win — because they’re in this together.
