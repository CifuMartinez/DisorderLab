The Delirium of Reason

Info
Geniuses and demons of modern science

Inspired by Maniac by Benjamín Labatut, this interactive project explores one of the darkest and most revealing episodes of modern science: the creation of the atomic bomb. Through a visual narrative with its own identity—featuring animation and creative code—it invites users to delve into the inner conflict of the scientists who, by pushing the boundaries of knowledge, were forced to confront its dual nature: the power to heal or to annihilate, to build or to destroy.

Worklife Balance

Info
Welcome to the future of separation

Worklife Balance is an immersive web experience inspired by Severance, simulating a corporate platform where wellbeing is only encouraged when it fuels productivity. Through serious-sounding tasks, cryptic motivation, and controlled relaxation, it explores the illusion of balance, the split between personal and work identity, and the silent demands of corporate culture—wrapped in a polished, ironic aesthetic.

"What if your work self didn't have to deal with your personal mess—and your personal self never had to think about work again?"
Worklife Balance Corporation
